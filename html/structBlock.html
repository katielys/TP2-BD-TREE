Descrição Detalhada

Disk block representation, it's important to say that we've made this structure to serve as block and bucket due the simplicity that it gave to us. This structure has a special attribute (nextBlock) that serves as pointer to the next linked block, simulating the scheme of buckets and overflow blocks found in literature.
